Digital Kiosk

Solution

Our initiative focuses on developing a user-friendly digital kiosk in public libraries to enhance refugee engagement with public housing schemes. This platform will enable refugees to complete documentation, research laws and regulations, and provide valuable feedback on their housing experiences. By incorporating multilingual support and regular updates, we aim to ensure accessibility and relevance for refugees. Library staff will receive training to assist refugees with any queries or digital literacy needs, fostering a more inclusive and supportive environment within public libraries for refugee communities.
