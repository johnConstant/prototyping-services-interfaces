COMMUNITY
AMBASSADOR

Connecting the Public Sector with their Users

Solution

My proposal involves recruiting community ambassadors from refugee populations to act as liaisons between decision-makers and their communities, fostering transparency and accountability in public service communication. Ambassadors will facilitate dialogue and collaboration, conveying community feedback through regular forums held in public spaces like libraries. To support their roles, comprehensive training programs on communication and advocacy will be provided. Measures to prevent burnout include rotational roles, and ongoing support programs will ensure sustained engagement. This initiative aims to establish a sustainable and inclusive framework for community ambassadors within public libraries.
